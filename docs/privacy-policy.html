Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Cofau ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Cofau mobile application ("App").

By using Cofau, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services.

a. Information You Provide

  • Name or username
  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Profile information (profile picture, bio)
  • Photos and videos you upload
  • Ratings, reviews, comments, and likes
  • Location information you voluntarily add to posts

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • App usage data (screens visited, interactions)
  • Log and diagnostic data for performance and error tracking

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your posts, reviews, ratings, and badges
  • Enable discovery of food and places
  • Calculate scores, rankings, and achievements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Photos, Videos, and User-Generated Content

  • Content you upload (photos, videos, reviews) is visible to other users based on app features.
  • You retain ownership of your content.
  • By posting, you grant Cofau a limited license to display and distribute your content within the app.

4. Camera and Photo Library Access

Cofau requests access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to:

  • Capture photos and videos of your food and dining experiences
  • Upload images from your photo library to include in your reviews and posts

We only access your camera or photo library when you actively choose to take or upload a photo/video. We do not access your camera or photos in the background.

5. Location Information

  • Location is optional and only used when you choose to tag a post or place.
  • We do not track your precise location in the background.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of users and Cofau

7. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices.
  • We use encryption and access controls to protect your data.
  • No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our safeguards.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Edit or delete your profile information
  • Remove your posts and reviews
  • Request account deletion (see below)

9. Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account at any time.

To delete your account:

  • Open the Cofau app
  • Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Confirm your decision

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile picture, bio) will be permanently deleted
  • Your reviews, ratings, photos, videos, and comments will be removed or anonymized
  • Your interactions (likes, follows) will be removed
  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone

Account deletion is processed immediately. In some cases, residual data may take up to 30 days to be fully removed from our backup systems.

If you are unable to access your account, you can request deletion by contacting us at the email address below.

10. Children's Privacy

Cofau is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
